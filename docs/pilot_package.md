# LeadClaw Pilot Validation Package

---

## Value Proposition

**LeadClaw helps small service businesses capture requests, follow up fast, book work, and get paid — without bloated software.**

---

## Pilot Pitch

You're already getting calls, texts, and emails from customers — but keeping track of who needs what, what's been quoted, and what's still unpaid is a mess. LeadClaw is a simple web app built for exactly this: customers submit a request, you see it instantly, book the job, complete it, invoice, and mark paid — all in one place on your phone. No CRM setup, no monthly seat fees, no learning curve. It takes about 5 minutes to start using it. We're looking for a handful of local service businesses to try it out and tell us honestly what works and what doesn't.

---

## Ideal First Pilot Users

Look for owners who:
- Run a solo or small-crew local service business (lawn care, cleaning, pressure washing, handyman, landscaping, gutter cleaning, etc.)
- Currently track jobs via text messages, a notebook, or a basic spreadsheet
- Get 5–30 service requests per month
- Do their own scheduling and invoicing (no dedicated office staff)
- Have a smartphone and are comfortable with simple web apps
- Are willing to give honest feedback after 1 week

Avoid (for now):
- Multi-location or franchise operations
- Businesses with complex dispatch / crew routing needs
- Anyone who needs QuickBooks integration immediately

---

## Feedback to Ask After 1 Week

- Did you actually use it for real requests? How many?
- Was the request form easy to share with customers?
- Did you get notified when a new request came in?
- Was the booking flow clear? Any confusion?
- Did the invoice / paid flow make sense?
- What did you do manually that you wished the app did?
- What feature did you expect to see that wasn't there?
- Would you keep using it if it stayed exactly as is?
- Did it replace anything you were doing before (texts, spreadsheet, notebook)?
- What would you pay for this, if anything?

---

## Usage Signals That Matter Most

- ≥1 real request submitted through `/request` (not a test)
- ≥1 request booked (owner took action)
- ≥1 job moved to "paid"
- Owner returns to the dashboard on day 3 and day 7
- Owner shares the `/request` link with a real customer
- Reminder badges prompted owner to take action
- Owner mentions it in conversation without being asked ("I just checked LeadClaw...")
